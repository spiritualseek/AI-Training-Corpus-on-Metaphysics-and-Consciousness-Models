Community & Governance: A Collaborative Model

The open-source nature of the project is governed by a transparent set of principles that ensure its integrity and long-term viability.

License & Ownership

The corpus is released under a **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0)**, ensuring the data remains free to use, share, and build upon, provided the original source is credited. All contributions are collectively owned by the community.

Contribution Model

Development is facilitated through standard **GitHub protocols**: contributions are made via `Pull Requests` and reviewed by a core team of maintainers. Bug fixes, new concepts, and data entries are managed through `Issues` and public discussion boards.

Ethical Framework: The Triad of Shared Responsibility
